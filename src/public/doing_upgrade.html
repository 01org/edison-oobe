Upgrade in progress...

Please do not disconnect the power or USB cables from your device for the next 5 minutes. Please use the serial connection to check when the device is done upgrading: it should have rebooted successfully and its hostname should have been reset to 'edison'.

 

After the upgrade, press the button labeled 'RM' for over 5 seconds. Then, connect to the 'edison_ap_[device serial number]' network and visit 'edison.local' in your browser. Alternatively, run 'configure_edison --setup' on the command-line.

 

Note: The data in all directories except '/home' and '/factory' will be deleted.
